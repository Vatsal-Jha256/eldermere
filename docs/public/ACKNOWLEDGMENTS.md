# Acknowledgments

Eldermere builds on a long history of text-based virtual worlds, tabletop role-playing games, and open-source web tools.

## MUD References

- MUD history and decades of room-based multiplayer worlds.
- TalesMUD, for showing how a modern browser MUD can combine web UI and text-first play.
- Evennia, for its content-first MUD architecture and builder-friendly concepts.
- DikuMUD, CircleMUD, LPMud, LDMud, FluffOS, Merc, and DGD as historical references for command loops, rooms, builders, drivers, and mudlibs.

## Source Material

Arthurian content is grounded in public-domain source material and original game writing. Modern adaptations may inspire tone or interest, but Eldermere does not copy their dialogue, scenes, character designs, branding, or unique plot inventions.
